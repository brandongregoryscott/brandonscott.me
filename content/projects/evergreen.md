Evergreen is Segment's open-source React design system with over 11,000 stars on Github. In just a short time, I became one of the [most frequent contributors](https://github.com/segmentio/evergreen/graphs/contributors) to the repository, pushing bug fixes, package upgrades, and new features, including an all-new [File Uploader](https://evergreen.segment.com/components/file-uploader) component. In addition to code contributions, I spent time responding to issues and questions on how to use specific components or features, like the theming architecture.
