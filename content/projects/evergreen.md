Evergreen is Segment's open-source React design system with over 11,000 stars on Github. I quickly became one of the [most frequent contributors](https://github.com/segmentio/evergreen/graphs/contributors) to the repository with bug fixes, package maintenance and tests, and new features, including an all-new [File Uploader](https://evergreen.segment.com/components/file-uploader) component. In addition to code contributions, I acted as a resource to the community - responding to questions, bug reports, and providing detailed guidance on some of the more technical parts of the system, such as theming.
